Home page

Importance of stock for the health of a company
Some say that sales are the “heart” of the company. In that sense we can compare the stock as the lungs of a company. Well-functioning lungs will allow the air to flow in and out with required frequency to keep you alive. If there is few air flowing in, you’ll have problems, but if there is too much air in and not flowing out, you’ll also have problems. The same problem will happen if there is “bad” air flowing in. In a company, in such comparison, we can say that goods in stock are like the air. It is very important to keep them flowing in and out in a constant and regular condition to keep the company alive. Lungs are the correct stock control and if they are not functioning well, the company will face hard time “breathing”. If there is too little stock, excess of stock or even too much “bad” stock (items that don’t sell regularly or with low profitability) a company may die. Our invitation to you is to check the health of your company’s stock and strengthen it to prepare it for higher climbs.

History of the Company

Aspects of stock control
Stocks play a very important role in every single company, from the smallest to the biggest, and it presents a series of challenge to those who administer it.
The stock control can be the difference between the growths of a company or simply be the cause of its bankruptcy. A well-controlled stock will bring many benefits to the company, among them are:
- Healthy cash flow level
when there is just enough goods in stock to keep your company running, without compromising too big portion of companys resources and allows the company to invest in other areas and allow its growth. - Stable supply to your customers, bringing more satisfaction and more customers by consequence.
You can attend your customers expectations in time and with efficiency and this will be the biggest advertisement tool your company can have and number of prospect customers will increase.
- Confidence to your sales team, that theyll attend their customers since, in our comparison, sales is a companys heart, with correct stock control, youll keep your sales team motivated and willing to look after new markets, because theyll trust your company will be able to attend them.
On the other hand, lack of good stock control can have the opposite effect:
- Shortage of supply to your customers
youll be putting too much effort to attend customer request and expend unnecessary money and effort to it, and customers satisfaction will drop and they may search for new supplier - Less motivation to sales team.
If there is a lack of stock and good attendance level to the customer, sales team tends to suffer immediately. That is because, customer will talk to sales team first and it is something that they are not be able to solve. Once situation remains, this can cause a great deal of demotivation to sales team and you may end up losing a good sales team to competitors.
- Cash flow problems, when you have too many assets stuck in stock.
High stock and low sales is a real nightmare that can be caused by lack of stock control. This will put extreme pressure on cash flow and can impact investments of the company and decision making processes
